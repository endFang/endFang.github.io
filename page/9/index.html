FFXV Royal Edition: main game

I dowloaded the pre-load but didn’t realize un-packing also needed almost 3 hours.
The game’s graphic quality (get out of my mind Kyle!) is almost the same to the PS4. But I think this is because I cannot tell the differences between them.

FFXV Royal Edition: Comrade

A youtuber comments that Comrade’s update is way more better than Royal Edition. I haven’t play this game more than 1 year, so I don’t know the differences.

Comrade’s story happens after Noctis disappeared in the crystal. I need to party with other players (or A.I.) to complete quests, which are basically hunts, and to progress the story. Quests’ rewards are Power and gils. Power is used to unlock quests, hunts, treasures, Sigils. etc..

The game’s plot is a a short story revealing how people defend the darkness when their King is gone and Kingsglaive’s betray and trial.

Weapons can be remodeled by adding specific stats. Some weapon can upgraded to Lv.99. Most are only Lv.60. Stats are customizable and decided by the player. I made a Wizard Shield with full elemental resistance and almost 200 magic. Due to this mechanism, the player can have varied builds.

Comrades is essentially another individual game in my opinion. It has completed system and story, a very short one thought. However, the matchmaking system does not work properly and the play base is extremely small. I’ve been playing Comrades since March 6th, but I barely meet other players. In addition, most of the content can be soloed with A.I.. I dare say people would rather play it offline.

FFXIV: Eureka, The Forbidden Land

My impression to Eureka: fate train.

Eureka is separated from the main game. Every player has elemental level in Eureka, which can be maxed to Lv.20. Random mobs drop Protean Crystal, while Fate drops Anemous Crystal. Crystals are currency for upgrading AF3 gears from iv.290 to ilv.350(weapon is 355).

After the first 3 days complain and whine , people figured out fate windows, spawning requirements, and Pazuzu feathers. An website tracking fate was also created. ( I hope I did.)

The optimal way of leveling and gaining crystals is fate train. Each Eureka instance allows 144 players to exist. Players gather together to do fate one by one. When they move from one place to another, an interesting “train” will appear.

People complained a lot about Eureka on forum, reddit, twitter, and any other platforms, but I do like Eureka more than other content. Eureka makes FFXIV become more alive and MMO-ish. Ever since 3.55, most players have optimal gears and tend to pull big in dungeons. They spend most of time to idle in Idyllshire and there main cities. It created an illusion of being comfortable and the impatience. It can be proved that in 4.0’s leveling dungeons, every tank tried to pull big and blame healers/dps for doing less damage, because that’s what they did in 3.4 - 3.5. Pulling big became an issue. Now we’re at end game again (3.2) and the same situation is happening. The game is too comfortable.

But Eureka is different. It has individual level system and enemies. Dangerous mobs dealing fatal damage are walking around near the beginning port. Players must level up first to explore the area. Even thought it is possible to sneak past mobs, players need to be certain level to use Aetheryte or summon mount. Among other things, flying mount is forbidden. In such a way, Eureka creates an beautiful but also dangerous area. Players need to cooperate to do Eureka, so the “fate train” begins.
I’ve never seen this many alive players ever since I’ve stop leveling by fate.

Another thing, this might the easiest 1st stage of relic so far. I only need to farming crystals.

Is Eureka that toxic and bad? No, I don’t think so, but I do think there are place for improvement.

Entry 2

Posted on 2018-01-28 Edited on 2019-04-23

去了 GGJ，和一群不认识的人在 48 小时内创作游戏。今年主题是 “Transmission”。

Arden 负责背景乐和音效。他拿出了一个接口和话筒，录制了敲击键盘和说话声，然后做成了合成乐。善用 Logic。我特别喜欢他制作的 7 个敲击键盘的声音。我把这七个声音随机播放，再写了一个自动打字的效果，做成了打字机或者 terminal 的样子。Howard 又加上了一个闪烁的光标特效。Mekin 说他觉得最终效果很有意思。

Tyler 教了我如何用 LoadAll 和 按钮自带的 OnClick()。好机智啊。

Mekin 提出了以博弈论为基础做一个文字冒险的生存游戏。同时他也负责 3D 建模。他做了一台飞船上的电脑。Tyler 与 Howard 配合做了一个特别酷炫的屏幕放大特效，又在屏幕背后的宇宙中做了星光。

Eddy 也有一部分美工，但是他大部分时间都在管理创作进程。在最后一个小时内他完成了初始画面的背景图。Arden 三次发现了单词拼写错误，他就改了三次。

Kyle 依据 Mekin 的主意写出 15 页的剧本，由 Tyler 订正后写了模板导入 Unity3D。
原本的计划是制作一个网络匹配后的冒险游戏，但是我们并没有办法在第二天中午之前想出网络连接的程序，只能做成一个单一客户端。Mekin 总是建议我们使用 TCP，实际上我们也这么做了，可是要控制的变量超出了我们的能力。

最可怕的是在最后一小时发现了一个严重 bug，不得已只能去掉了光标效果才能让游戏运行。

特别有意思的是，Kyle 在最后一小时拉上了 Mekin 和 Arden，三人一起又做了一个超级简单的射击游戏“One Shot”。在展览上意外获得好评。Arden 制作的洗脑循环 “one shot” 十分魔性，每次听到我都特别想笑。

我特别佩服 Tyler，写起代码来啪啪的，而且总是能够早起。解决问题也特别快。我也特别佩服 Kyle，能够写出不同文风的剧本。
最后是 Electrico 拿了两个奖项。Electrico 是真的好玩啊。三人一起合作跨越障碍抵达终点。我有幸坐在了 Electrico 的旁边看到了整个游戏的诞生。

我还看到了好多 Apple Watch。

顺带，The Nash Transmission.
